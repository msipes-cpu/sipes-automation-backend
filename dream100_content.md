## Dossier 1: Zappitell Law Firm
**Target**: David Zappitell (Managing Partner)

### Slide 1: The Current State (Problem)
**Title**: The Hidden Cost of "Traditional" Intake at Zappitell Law Firm
**Body**: 
David, looking at your firm's growth (based on LinkedIn data), your team is likely spending 20-30 hours a week on:
- Manual client intake forms.
- Chasing signatures on retainers.
- Collating case files from email attachments.

**Metric**: This "admin drag" is costing you roughly $2,500/month in billable capacity per paralegal.

### Slide 2: The Solution (Automation Pilot)
**Title**: Reclaiming 50% of That Time
**Body**:
We propose a 30-day pilot to automate **Client Intake**.
1.  **Lead comes in** (Website/Phone).
2.  **Auto-Qualify**: System checks conflict of interest & case viability.
3.  **Auto-Retainer**: Sends DocuSign immediately if qualified.
4.  **Auto-File**: Create matter in Practice Management software & Google Drive.

### Slide 3: The "No-Brainer" Offer
**Title**: Let Me Build This For Free
**Body**:
I am selecting 5 firms for a free pilot.
- I build this specific Intake Automation for you.
- You use it for 30 days.
- Cost: $0.
- Ask: Feedback & a Testimonial if it works.

**Target**: Summer Dierks (Managing Partner)

All I ask is that you test drive it.
