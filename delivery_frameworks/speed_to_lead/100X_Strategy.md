## 1. The Strategy: "Don't Just Ping. Perform."

The standard Speed-to-Lead install ensures the lead is *contacted*. The 100X Upgrade ensures the lead is *qualified, enriched, and booked*.

### Level 1: Instant Voice AI (The Logic Leap)
*   **The Problem:** SMS is great, but phone calls convert 10x better. Sales reps hate making calls instantly.
*   **The Solution:** We integrate **Retell AI** or **Vapi**.
*   **The Workflow:**
    1.  Lead submits form.
    2.  System waits 30 seconds (to simulate human).
    3.  AI calls the lead.
    4.  AI Script: *"Hey, this is Sarah from [Agency]. Can I ask you 2 quick questions to see if we can help?"*
    5.  **Booked:** If qualified, AI transfers to a human OR books a meeting on Calendly.

### Level 2: Lead Enrichment (The Intelligence Layer)
*   **The Problem:** Sales reps waste time calling "junk" leads or researching prospects.
*   **The Solution:** We integrate **Apollo** or **Clearbit**.
*   **The Workflow:**
    1.  Lead enters email: `john@acme.com`
    2.  We ping Apollo API.
    3.  We retrieve: *Title (VP Sales), Revenue ($50M), LinkedIn URL, Location*.

### Level 3: Omni-Channel Persistence (The Follow-Up)
*   **The Problem:** Most reps give up after 1 try.
*   **The Solution:** A 7-day automated "Squeeze Sequence".
*   **The Workflow:**
